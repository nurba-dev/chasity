about us

Welcome to Chicity Builders, where craftsmanship meets excellence.

At Chicity Builders, we are a team of dedicated experts who are passionate about our craft. Our artisans and craftsmen bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every project, ensuring exceptional results that exceed our clients' expectations.

Why you should choose us?

Uncompromising Quality

We are committed to delivering top-notch craftsmanship and exceptional quality in every project. Our skilled team of experts takes great pride in their work, ensuring that every detail is meticulously attended to, resulting in outstanding results that exceed expectations.

Extensive Expertise

With years of experience in the industry, we have honed our skills and expertise across a wide range of fine works. From painting and decorating to tiling, flooring, carpentry, and more, our versatile team possesses the knowledge and proficiency to handle any project with precision and professionalism.

Client-Centric Approach

We prioritize our clients and their satisfaction above all else. We believe in fostering strong relationships built on trust, open communication, and personalized attention. Our dedicated team listens to your needs, provides expert guidance, and collaborates closely with you throughout the process, ensuring that your vision is brought to life seamlessly and to your utmost satisfaction.

What we do ?

Painting
and decorating

Our skilled team of professionals utilizes their expertise and a keen eye for detail to deliver flawless painting and decorating services, ensuring precise application, seamless finishes, and a perfect blend of colors and textures that enhance the aesthetic appeal of your space.

Tiling

With meticulous precision, our experienced craftsmen expertly install ceramic, porcelain, and various types of tiles, creating stunning walls and floors. We ensure precise alignment, uniform grouting, and a smooth, durable surface that adds both style and functionality to your home or business.

Flooring

From hardwood to laminate, vinyl, and other flooring materials, our team has the knowledge and skill to install them with exceptional craftsmanship. We focus on proper preparation, precise measurements, and expert installation techniques, resulting in beautiful, long-lasting floors that elevate the overall ambiance of your space.

Carpentry

Our talented carpenters bring their expertise to every project, whether it's installing doors, windows, or frames. With precision and attention to detail, we deliver finely crafted wooden structures that enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your property.

Plastering

Our skilled plasterers meticulously apply plaster and other materials to walls and ceilings, creating a smooth, flawless finish. With their expertise, they ensure that every surface is perfectly prepared and finished, resulting in visually appealing and long-lasting results.

Electrical
and plumbing work

Our team of licensed professionals handles electrical and plumbing installations and repairs with utmost professionalism. With a focus on safety and efficiency, we ensure that your lighting fixtures, switches, sockets, and plumbing systems are installed or repaired to the highest standards, providing reliable functionality for your property.

HVAC

Our experienced HVAC technicians specialize in installing and repairing heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. With their knowledge and expertise, they ensure optimal climate control and comfort, delivering efficient and reliable HVAC solutions tailored to your specific needs.

Waterproofing

Our skilled professionals utilize high-quality waterproofing materials and techniques to protect your surfaces from water damage. With meticulous attention to detail, we apply waterproof coatings and treatments, preventing moisture penetration and ensuring the longevity and durability of your property.
